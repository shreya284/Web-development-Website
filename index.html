Discover and Explore the various and trending technologies used in the creation of the future websites for the mankind's betterment. Front-End and Back-End, both have vital roles to being completely known as a Full-stack Development or even referably known as Web-Design, where only the front-end is taken into usage and implementation.

Summary

Web Development and Web Design are both different parts of skill-interests. Here, as you can see, Web Development involves also the back-end part of the website including the front-end part too, thereby making it a full-stack developed website/webpage.

Web Design only includes the making of the front-part of the website, by the usage of front-end languages such as:HTML,CSS,Javascript,Jquery and much more. It also concerns the end consumer, who are the clients(including as we us) to browse and search into anything at the front-side of the webpage/website.

Languages

Front-End languages are mainly designed to give the look and feel of a webpage. They also represent and show the beautiful structural design look of any webpage/website by using the three main languages such as:HTML,CSS,Javascript and even includingBootstrap,JQuery etc.

Wheras, Coming to the Back-end part, They feature as the backbone of a website/webpage to support the functionality of the website by using SQL,Python,Ruby,PHP etc. One thing to add on is that, back-end part of a website can not been shown,and is mainly hidden from the user(client).
